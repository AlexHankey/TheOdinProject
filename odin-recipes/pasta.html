Pasta Recipe

Original Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound tomatos
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1/2 cup onion
  • 1/2 cup garlic
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 1/2 cup tomato paste
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1/2 cup bread crumbs
  • 1/2 cup mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup basil
  • 1/2 cup oregano
  • 1/2 cup salt
  • 1/2 cup pepper
  • 1/2 cup olive oil

Directions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
  2. In a large bowl, combine tomatos, ground beef, onion, garlic, tomato sauce, tomato paste, water, bread crumbs, mozzarella cheese, parmesan cheese, basil, oregano, salt, and pepper.
